Random Password Generator

This is a random password generator as well as a security question answer generator. The password generates 16 character, randomized passwords with both upper and lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols. The security answer generator has random words or phrases that you can use. Some of the words or phrases may sound random or ridiculous. They are supposed to :). You want your security answers to be as random or ridiculous as possible to make them hard to guess by bad actors. So enjoy :)
